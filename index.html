Who I Am

My journey into software engineering is one that is rather unconventional and length, as I was only introduced to the filed of computer science in my last semester of college. I started my jourey as a double major in psychology and kinesiology. Where not long after I realized that the sole reason to my decision and interest in these fields is because of childhood trauma. As I became aware of this, I defaulted back to what I was comfortable with, growing up in a business family, what I was comfortable with, was business. Which is what I finished and got my degreee in. However, upon graduation, as I thought about what I wanted to accomplish with my degree as well as with my life, I came to a realization that, in order for myself to be put in a position where I would be able to make more of a global impact, I must immerse myself into change that is happening outside the confines of my comfort zone. Evalutating how the world is evolving and how technology is often at the forefront of it. Started my jourey to becoming a software engineer.
